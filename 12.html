Globally, there has been real and significant change as a result of the internet amplification of both micro and macro politics. Social media has boosted activism, and 67% of Gen Zs and Millennials say they must now embrace campaigns in their life and be more engaged since apathy is no longer an option. This also applies to the music people listen to and how it may inform and inspire them. These generations not only anticipate good change, but also have the resources to bring it about on their own.
