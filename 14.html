According to 56% of Gen Zs and Millennials who responded to a Spotify poll, there is too much visual stimulation in our everyday lives, and music and podcasts are their escape. For many people, having a decent set of earbuds or headphones is just as crucial as having a smartphone, and the development of smart speakers and linked automobiles is enveloping us in sound like never before, tailoring it to our needs - from resting to working out, a soundtrack for everything.
